Audience

As a MICCAI attendee, you are more than prepared to make part of the tutorial. The event is aimed at a broad audience within the MICCAI community. This includes

  • Researchers and students interested in learning more about Data-Centric and Continual AI and how it can empower their research, with practical experience in model development.
  • Industry practitioners and medical professionals who could benefit from adopting the presented tools.
  • Project stakeholders, ethicists, policy-makers, and regulators, who wish to understand how Data-Centric and Continual AI can imbue transparency and accountability — especially relevant in the medical context — and what are the regulatory implications for updating software products.

Instructions

Please bring your laptop which can access Google Colab in a webbrowser. The laptop does not need to fulfill any special technical specifications or be suitable for training Deep Learning models. If you would like to participate but cannot bring your laptop this will still be fine, as participants will work in groups.
