Audience

As a MICCAI attendee, you are more than prepared to make part of the tutorial. The event is aimed at a broad audience within the MICCAI community. This includes researchers with experience training and validating machine learning solutions, clinicians who interact with (or are open to incorporating) decision support systems in their workflow, and company representatives interested in releasing software solutions that learn over time. We are particularly interested in benefiting from a wide range of perspectives.

Instructions

Please bring your laptop which can access Google Colab in a webbrowser. The laptop does not need to fulfill any special technical specifications or be suitable for training Deep Learning models. If you would like to participate but cannot bring your laptop this will still be fine, as participants will work in groups.
